Who is Listables?

Started in November 2016, Listables is a fast growing social media outlet for people that enjoy List based content. We take pride in providing the content right to the fan's newsfeed rather than sending the user to a website to enjoy our quality content. We strive to build a friendly and strong community with a brand that is easily recognizable. We strive to continue to bring only the best list content to our fans.
